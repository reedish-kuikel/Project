Animated movies are films that use animation techniques to bring stories and characters to life, often featuring vibrant visuals and imaginative worlds. These films span a variety of genres, from adventure and fantasy to comedy and drama, and are designed to engage both children and adults with their creativity, humor, and emotional depth. Whether through traditional hand-drawn animation, computer-generated imagery (CGI), or stop-motion, animated movies offer a unique and visually captivating experience.
